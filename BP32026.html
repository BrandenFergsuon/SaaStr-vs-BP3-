BP3 AI Strategy Assessment vs SaaStr Playbook
Competitive Analysis & Implementation Roadmap

Executive Summary

SaaStr's Model: Replaced 10 human SDRs/AEs with 20 AI agents managed by 1.2 FTE, maintaining $8M+ ARR. Total cost: approximately $500K/year for agent stack.

BP3's Current State: Salesforce CRM with integrated HubSpot marketing automation + native Breeze AI agents + 1 BDR executing automated sequences through HubSpot + Dealfront (lead intelligence). Additional automation includes website support bot trained on company content and custom workflows incorporating LLMs for digital lead management.

Key Gap: While BP3 has foundational AI automation in place, we are not yet deploying specialized outbound/inbound agents at the scale demonstrated by SaaStr. Our current BDR automation through HubSpot sequences provides baseline efficiency but lacks the hyper-personalization and 24/7 capabilities of dedicated AI agents.

Monitoring & Analytics: BP3 utilizes custom dashboards in Salesforce and HubSpot to monitor automation performance, with ability to amend workflows and sequences as needed to optimize outcomes.
